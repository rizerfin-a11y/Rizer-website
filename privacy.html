Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 11, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information that you explicitly provide. Specifically, when you connect your Google Calendar, we receive an OAuth token that allows us to sync your tasks. This data is stored locally in your browser's LocalStorage and is never uploaded to our servers.

2. Use of Google User Data

We use Google Calendar data exclusively to add, update, and delete tasks as requested by you within the app. We do not share, sell, or use this data for any marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Local Storage

All your tasks, goals, and settings are stored locally on your device. Clearing your browser cache or data will remove this information.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google Identity Services for authentication. Their use of your data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Contact

For any questions regarding your privacy, please contact the developer via the GitHub repository.
